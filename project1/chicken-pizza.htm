Dr. Oetker FunFoods Pizza Topping All in One (2 tbsp.)

How to Prepare:

Prep:
40 min
1

Preheat oven to 200°C.

2

In a bowl add chicken cubes, oil, salt and black pepper and marinate for 30 minutes.

3

Spread pizza topping on pizza base. Place capsicum, onion, tomato, olives and marinated chicken. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top to cover the vegetables and chicken.

4

Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese melts and pizza bases turns light brown.

5

 Cut pizza into 4 pieces and serve.
